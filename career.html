Presidential Career

Trumps first term 2017-2021

During his first term, President Donald Trump enacted the Tax Cuts and Jobs Act, aiming to stimulate economic growth through tax reforms. He also oversaw the elimination of ISIS leader Abu Bakr al-Baghdadi, marking a significant counterterrorism achievement.

Trumps second term current

In his current term, President Donald Trump has focused on administrative restructuring and efficiency. He established the Department of Government Efficiency (DOGE) under Elon Musk, aiming to reduce federal spending, leading to significant agency layoffs.
